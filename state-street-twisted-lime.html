Twisted Lime Product Photos

State Street Beard Company was releasing its first limited edition scent, Twisted Lime. State Street wanted product photos that expressed the lime theme, while keeping in mind it was for the spring season. State Street also required shadows within the product photos. I used a pink-quartz background. I felt it reflected the idea of a cutting board, and was the brightest option that I had to choose from. Keeping the background light-colored kept the lime mainstage without it drowning into the background. I used freshly cut limes using the shine of the juice seeping out to express the freshness this scent would bring. I took 10 photos for State Street, one for each product, and for their combos, giving each one the freshly lime theme.
